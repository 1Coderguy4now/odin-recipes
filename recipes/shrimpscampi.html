Shrimp Scampi

Description

This recipe consists of an infusion of both citrus and creamy flavors. If you like shrimp and a little adventure then this recipe is right for you.

The best part is that you won't need vary many ingredients for this recipe, most likely you already most of the ingredients at home.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Extra virgin oil
  • White wine
  • Lemon juice
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Prep ahead: Have all of your ingredients prepared and ready to cook before heating your skillet.
  2. Cook the pasta first: Boil the pasta in well-salted water, drain, and hold aside before cooking the shrimp scampi.
  3. Don't overcook the shrimp: It takes just a couple of minutes to cook raw shrimp

Follow these simple steps and enjoy!
