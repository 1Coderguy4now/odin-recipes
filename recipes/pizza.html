Pizza

Description

If you are looking for a quick and easy pizza at home then this is your recipe

The ingredients are easily obtainable and you probably already have them handy

  • Bread Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients in a good size bowl
  2. Prepare your surface with oil
  3. Shape the dough
  4. Let the dough rest
  5. Transfer dough to a pizza pan
  6. Add your favorite toppings
  7. Bake in oven at 340 degrees until golden brown for about 15 to 20 minutes

Now you can make your own pizza at the fraction of the cost. Enjoy!
